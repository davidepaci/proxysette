Audiotap 2.2
-----------

  Source at https://github.com/fabzzap/audiotap (tag audiotap-2.2)
  Distributed under the GNU General Public License, see LICENSE.TXT

  Source at https://github.com/fabzzap/libtap (tag libtap-2.2)
  Distributed under the GNU Lesser General Public License, see
  LESSER-LICENSE.TXT
* tapdecoder.dll, a generator of variable-length waves

  LESSER-LICENSE.TXT
